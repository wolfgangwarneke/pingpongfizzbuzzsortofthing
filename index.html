Tutorial:

Welcome! Ping pong counts by ones, but the two multiples will change the number to "ping" or "pong". If the number has both multiples it becomes both "ping" and "pong"... Pingpong! In arcade mode of Ping Pong Pingpong you will navigate your way through the game by predicting whether the next number will actually be a number or whether it will change to "ping", "pong", "pingpong". But don't get it wrong or you'll lose a life! (Don't worry you get 3.)
